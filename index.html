RACE

RACING

We are Team Velox, a STEM Racing Team based in Stuyvesant High School, founded in 2025. Our team is composed of six passionate high school students who are willing to learn. Our teams mission is to gain real-life skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and leadership and proudly represent our school at the National Finals.

02

CREATE

PROCESS

Brainstorm

Our engineers come together to brainstorm ideas and concepts for the design of the car. During this stage, we focus on creativity, aerodynamics, and regulation.

Design

With the ideas, we build the 3D model of the car using CAD (Computer Aid Design) software. Our engineers ensure that every requirement of the Technical Regulations is met. Using CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics), we can analyze the airflow around our car to optimize the aerodynamic performance of the car.

Manufacture/Assembly

Using CNC machining and 3D printing, our team manufactures the body, wings, wheels, and all necessary components to bring the car to life. Our engineers sand each component diligently to ensure smooth assembly. Finally, we paint the car to reflect our team's unique identity.

Test

After the assembly of the car, we perform physical tests to evaluate the cars performance. We observe the friction, wheel alignment, and overall speed and use this data to improve our car.
